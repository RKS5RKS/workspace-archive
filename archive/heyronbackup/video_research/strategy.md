### Prompt Framework That Works
- [Style] + [Subject] + [Setting] + [Mood] + [Quality tags]
- Avoid: wet, nude, naked, see-through, sensual, hot tub, bra, bikini
- Use instead: spa pool, minimalist, serene, editorial, artistic

### What Gets Rejected (Don't Use)
- "hot tub" → use "spa pool"
- "wet" alone → use "water dripping" or skip
- "braless/naked/nude" → triggers instant rejection
- "sensual/erotic" → rejected
- "see-through/transparent" → rejected
- More than 2 women → higher rejection rate
- "plus sized" + certain settings → triggers
