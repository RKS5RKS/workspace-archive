In just a few months, NoMark's social media videos have drawn more than **500,000 followers**, who watch the anonymous character recover a stolen bike, break up a fight and battle an internet nemesis.

NoMark hoped he'd made an impression beyond virtual "likes" as he gave the guys farewell fist bumps. "It feels good for them to appreciate me, obviously, but more than that, I like hearing like that they like to do good stuff, too," he said. Then he set off into the night, his shadowy figure enveloped in darkness.

NoMark was photographed on Minneapolis' Stone Arch Bridge.
