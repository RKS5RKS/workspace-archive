# Business Planning Prompts Section

## 1. Business Plan Components

###Executive Summary Generator
```
Create an executive summary for [business idea] including:
- Business description
- Problem statement
- Solution/offer
- Target market
- Revenue model
- Growth strategy
- Ask (if seeking funding)

Write in professional investor-ready tone. 500-750 words.
```

### Market Analysis Framework
```
Conduct a comprehensive market analysis for [product/service]:
- TAM (Total Addressable Market) with sources
- SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)
- SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)
- Market growth rate (CAGR)
- Key trends
- Competitive landscape

Include 5-7 competitors with positioning and weaknesses
```

### Competitive Analysis Matrix
```
Create a competitive analysis for [business type]:
For each of 10 competitors provide:
- Positioning statement
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Pricing
- Marketing approach
- Distribution channels

Then identify 3-5 white space opportunities
```

## 2. Financial Projections

### 3-Year Financial Model
```
Build a 3-year financial model for [business idea]:

Year 1 (Monthly for 12 months):
- Revenue projections by month
- Customer acquisition cost
- Churn rate assumption
- Gross margin
- Operating expenses breakdown
- EBITDA

Repeat for Years 2 and 3 with growth assumptions stated.

Include sensitivity analysis for conservative, base, and optimistic scenarios.
```

### Pricing Strategy
```
Develop a pricing strategy for [product/service]:
- Cost-plus calculation
- Value-based pricing rationale
- Competitive price positioning
- Tiered pricing structure (if applicable)
- Discount strategy
- Anchor pricing (show original vs. discounted)

Include psychological pricing tactics to use
```

### Unit Economics Calculator
```
Calculate unit economics for [business]:
- Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- LTV:CAC ratio
- Payback period
- Gross margin per customer
- Break-even analysis

Provide formulas and explain each metric
```
