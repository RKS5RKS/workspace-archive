# 10 Money‑Making Ideas Under $200

## 1. AI‑Powered Content Rewrite Service
**Description:** Offer a fast content rewriting/redrafting service for blogs, newsletters, or social media using AI. Clients submit drafts; you use an AI model to improve tone, SEO, and readability.
**Startup Cost:** $0‑$30 (subscription to an affordable AI API or free tier).
**Potential Income:** $15‑$50 per piece; 10‑20 pieces/week → $150‑$1000/month.
**How to Execute:**
1. Set up a simple website or landing page (e.g., Carrd). 
2. Connect to an AI text‑generation API (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) with a paid plan if needed. 
3. Advertise on freelance platforms (Fiverr, Upwork) and niche forums. 
4. Deliver revised content via email/Google Docs.

## 2. Niche Blog with AI‑Generated Articles
**Description:** Create a small niche blog (e.g., “Urban Gardening in Minneapolis”) and populate it with AI‑written articles, then monetize via ads and affiliate links.
**Startup Cost:** $50 (domain + cheap hosting).
**Potential Income:** $50‑$300/month from ads/affiliates after traffic builds.
**How to Execute:**
1. Choose a micro‑niche with low competition. 
2. Use AI to generate 5‑10 high‑quality posts per week. 
3. Optimize SEO (keyword research tools, free plugins). 
4. Sign up for Google AdSense and relevant affiliate programs.

**Startup Cost:** $0‑$20 (Mailchimp free tier, AI API if needed).

3. Publish via a free email service, upsell premium tier.
