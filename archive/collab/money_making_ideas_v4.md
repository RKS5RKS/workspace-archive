## 15 Money‑Making Ideas Under $200

1. **AI‑Powered Resume Tweaker** – Offer a service that uses free LLM APIs (or a low‑cost plan) to rewrite and keyword‑optimize resumes for job seekers. Market on LinkedIn groups and local career centers.

2. **Micro‑Niche TikTok/YouTube Shorts Channel** – Create short‑form videos on a hyper‑specific hobby (e.g., “DIY tiny‑home accessories”). Use a $100 budget for a decent phone tripod, basic lighting, and a royalty‑free music subscription. Monetize via brand deals and affiliate links.

3. **Local “Pet‑Check‑In” Service** – Advertise on Nextdoor or Facebook Marketplace to walk, feed, and give a quick photo update for nearby pet owners who travel short distances. Buy inexpensive leashes and a portable water bottle for supplies.

4. **Custom Voice‑Over Scripts for TikTok Influencers** – Offer rapid, 30‑second voice‑over scripts using a free text‑to‑speech tool (or a low‑cost subscription). Charge per script; use a $50 mic upgrade for better quality.

5. **Cold‑Email Template Builder for Small E‑com Stores** – Design ready‑to‑use email sequences (welcome, cart‑abandon, post‑purchase). Sell bundles on Gumroad; spend $30 on a simple landing page builder.

6. **Localized SEO Audit for Small Restaurants** – Use free SEO tools (Google Search Console, Ubersuggest free tier) to generate a report on local search visibility. Charge $75 per audit and offer a follow‑up implementation service.

7. **Translation Proofreading Service** – For non‑native speakers who use machine translation (Google Translate, DeepL free). Offer quick proofreading for $10‑$15 per 500 words. Use a $20 subscription to a grammar checker.

8. **AI‑Generated Social Media Caption Pack** – Create packs of 30 captions for Instagram reels in a niche (e.g., fitness coaches). Use a free AI writer, format in a PDF, and sell on Etsy for $12.

9. **Home‑Office Ergonomics Consultation (Virtual)** – Offer 30‑minute video calls to assess desk setups and recommend inexpensive adjustments (monitor risers, chair cushions). Market via local coworking spaces.

10. **Neighborhood “Forgotten Items” Finder** – Advertise to help busy homeowners locate lost items (keys, remote controls) using a simple checklist and a $15 printed flyer. Charge $20 per session.

11. **Batch Video Script Service for Local Real Estate Agents** – Write 5‑minute video scripts for property walkthroughs. Use a $25 subscription to a script‑writing AI tool and sell each script for $30.

12. **Printable Planner Templates for Niche Audiences** – Design printable daily or habit trackers for specific groups (e.g., “Gardening Hobbyists”). Use Canva free version, sell PDFs on Shopify or Gumroad.

13. **Small‑Biz Automation Checklist** – Compile a step‑by‑step guide to automate email marketing, invoicing, and social posting using free or low‑cost tools (Zapier free tier, Mailchimp free). Charge $50 per download.

14. **Virtual “Tech‑Help Hour” for Seniors** – Host weekly Zoom sessions (max 5 participants) to troubleshoot common device issues. Charge $10 per person per session; use a $20 subscription to a screen‑sharing tool if needed.

15. **Local “Green‑Cleaning” Referral Service** – Curate a list of eco‑friendly cleaning services in Minneapolis, charge businesses a $30 referral fee per client. Use $40 for printed flyers and online ads.

*All ideas are designed to be launched with $200 or less in startup costs and focus on solving a clear problem people are willing to pay for.*
